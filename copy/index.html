Pet Protectors Animal Shelter

Saving, rehabilitating, and protecting the lives of at-risk animals.

Our goal at Pet Protectors is to provide care for animals who have been or are at risk for abuse, rehabilitating them, and integrating them into a family in which they can thrive.

Dogs
Cats
